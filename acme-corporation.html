01Program Overview

How the program works

Four simple steps to save on your prescriptions and earn cash back.

  1. 01

    Check if your drug is available

    Use the drug lookup tool on this page or browse costplusdrugs.com to see if your medication is in the Cost Plus formulary. Most common generics are available at dramatically lower prices.

  2. 02

    Order directly on Cost Plus Drugs

    Visit costplusdrugs.com, create a free account, and order your prescription. You’ll pay out of pocket at checkout — the prices are already a fraction of what you’d pay at a traditional pharmacy.

  3. 03

    Submit your receipt

    After your order arrives, upload your Cost Plus receipt through our simple reimbursement form. Include the order confirmation showing the drug name, quantity, and amount paid.

  4. 04

    Get fully reimbursed + earn a reward

    Maverick processes your claim within 3 business days. You’ll receive a payment for 100% of your out-of-pocket cost (prescription + shipping) PLUS a 10% savings reward — up to $50 per fill.

03Getting Started

How to register on Cost Plus Drugs

Setting up your Cost Plus Drugs account takes about 5 minutes.

  1. 01

    Go to costplusdrugs.com

    Open costplusdrugs.com in your browser. The site is available on desktop and mobile.

  2. 02

    Create a free account

    Click “Sign Up” and enter your name, email address, date of birth, and shipping address. There is no membership fee — the account is completely free.

  3. 03

    Search for your medication

    Use the search bar to find your drug by name. You can search by brand name or generic name. Select the correct dosage and quantity.

  4. 04

    Complete your order

    Add to cart and checkout. You’ll pay with a credit or debit card. Your prescription will be reviewed by a licensed pharmacist before shipping — no physical prescription card needed for most generics.

  5. 05

    Receive your order

    Medications typically arrive in 4–7 business days. Keep your order confirmation email — you’ll need it to submit your reimbursement request.

Important: keep your receipts

Save your Cost Plus order confirmation email after every purchase. You’ll upload it when submitting your reimbursement. Without a receipt, we cannot process your claim.

04Reimbursement

How to submit a receipt

The reimbursement process is simple — no paper forms, no mailing anything.

  1. 01

    Gather your Cost Plus receipt

    After your order arrives, locate your order confirmation email from Cost Plus Drugs. It shows your name, drug name, quantity, amount paid, and shipping cost.

  2. 02

    Open the reimbursement form

    Click the Submit a Claim button below. You’ll be taken to a secure online form managed by Maverick Administrators.

  3. 03

    Fill in your information

    Enter your name, email address, employer name, and the details from your receipt: drug name, date of purchase, prescription amount, and shipping amount.

  4. 04

    Upload your receipt

    Attach a photo or PDF of your Cost Plus order confirmation. A screenshot from your email is fine — just make sure the drug name, date, and total amount are clearly visible.

  5. 05

    Submit and wait for confirmation

    Once submitted, you’ll receive an email confirmation within minutes. Maverick will review your claim within 3 business days and notify you of the outcome.

What to include in your submission

  • Your full name and email address
  • Your employer’s name
  • Drug name and dosage
  • Date of purchase
  • Prescription amount (from Cost Plus receipt)
  • Shipping amount (from Cost Plus receipt)
  • Receipt file (PDF or photo — order confirmation email is fine)

05Getting Paid

How and when you receive your reimbursement

Payments are processed through Tremendous — a secure payment platform that lets you choose exactly how you want to receive your money.

  1. 01

    Claim submitted

    Day 0 — you submit your receipt.

  2. 02

    Under review

    Within 3 business days.

  3. 03

    Payment sent

    Email from Tremendous to choose payment method.

  4. 04

    Funds received

    Same day to 2–4 days depending on method.
